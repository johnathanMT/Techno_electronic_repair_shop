Meet the expert

Ko Myo Thant — Head Technician

Brief Introduction

Ko Myo Thant has over 15 years of hands-on experience repairing every kind of television and monitor — from older Plasma and LCD sets to the newest Android and Smart TVs. He specialises in board-level and backlight repair, personally inspects every device, and stands behind each fix with a 1-year warranty.

Our Story

Shop History

TECHNO MYO THANT began in 2010 as a small one-bench workshop repairing neighbourhood televisions. Word spread quickly because customers valued the honest advice and repairs that actually lasted.

Today the shop is a trusted local name for TV and home-electronics repair, serving hundreds of families and businesses every month — but our promise is unchanged: treat every device like it is our own.
